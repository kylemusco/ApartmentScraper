Courthouse

Courthouse Plaza Apartments is in the heart of Arlington's Courthouse urban village, neighboring D.C.'s Georgetown neighborhood and Arlington's extremely popular Rosslyn and Clarendon areas. Courthouse is considered the civic center of town, close to the namesake Arlington County Courthouse and plenty of entertainment, including a nearby movie theater, fitness centers and top-notch restaurants that feature a variety of cuisines from around the world. No matter what you're in the mood for, Courthouse and its neighboring urban villages have it covered. Visit us today and experience all the Arlington adventures that await!
